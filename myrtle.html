Welcome to
Myrtle Trading Co.

Cultivating the very best in Australian native botanicals.

We grow, manage, and produce superior quality organic native botanicals on the lands of the Bundjalung people, the ancient, sub-tropical rainforests of the Northern Rivers hinterland and the original home of the Lemon Myrtle.

The history and provenance of Lemon Myrtle is deeply rooted in the The Northern Rivers – the Bundjalung nation. The terroir of this region defines Lemon Myrtle.

About us

MyrtleCo is a native botanicals company that’s 100 per cent Australian-owned, grown, and operated. One of only a few commercial-scale suppliers of Lemon Myrtle globally, we work with local farmers and growers to provide the world with the highest quality, organic native botanicals grown in the rich, volcanic soils of the Northern Rivers.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the power of working together to protect people and the planet. We believe in creating local jobs, building strong communities, and treating the land the best way we know how. We’re entirely organic and carefully follow regenerative agricultural principles to ensure we’re doing all we can to improve soil health and enhance biodiversity. What’s more, we use modern manufacturing technologies, which enable us to deliver botanicals of the very highest quality.
